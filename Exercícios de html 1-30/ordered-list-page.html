Baking a Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking pan.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt) in a large bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients (eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract).
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, and mix until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
